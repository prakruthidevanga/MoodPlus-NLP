How it works
Emotion detection engine

Each of the 13 emotions has a curated keyword lexicon. When you click Analyze, your text is matched against all lexicons simultaneously. Scores are weighted by keyword strength and normalized into probabilities.

Contextual boosters — exclamation marks, question marks, ALL CAPS, and negations (don't, can't, never) — adjust scores for more accurate results.
